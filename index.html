WHAT IS LUPUS?

Understanding the condition

Lupus is a chronic autoimmune disease in which the immune system attacks thebody's own healthy tissue. It can affect the skin, joints, kidneys, heart, lungs, and brain — often mimicking other conditions, making early diagnosis difficult.

Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower every person affected by lupus in Kenya — patient, family member, or caregiver — with access to accurate information, compassionate community, and the connections needed to advocate for quality care.

Vision

A Kenya where lupus is detected early, understood widely, and no longer a lonely diagnosis — where every patient has a community, and every caregiver is supported and seen.
